Visit New York!

New York is a state in the North Eastern United States. With a population of approximately 19.6 million residents, New York is the fourth most populous state in the country.
We're famous for landmarks such as Times Square, Central Park, and Grand Central Terminal, and home to the luxurious Ivy League, Columbia University and Cornell University.
